CHECKMATE

Pick Your Version

Three AI tools. Three unique takes. One game about restroom etiquette.

It started with Kiro and Claude Sonnet 4.5. That was my first real taste of Vibe Coding. Built a few other apps after that, then came back and gave Gemini 3.1 Pro and GPT-5.4 the same challenge: build your own version of the original.

The prompt was simple:

"Research Checkmate thoroughly to understand the game, the mission, the mechanics and the UX. Create your own rendition of the game with your own personality. You can either use the Vanilla UX or install any other packages for this."

Two new editions came out of that single prompt. They're not perfect, but they're fun in their own way. Give them a go — though fair warning, you'll probably get bored pretty quick.
